# 游늵 B1.3: Radio Silence Compliance

**Associated Hypothesis:** `10_hypotheses/B_external_threats.md#b1-the-dark-forest-trigger`

**Core Question:** Are external transmissions revealing our technological advancement and creating detectability risks?

---

## 1. Measurement Definition

### What We're Tracking
The **Radio Silence Compliance** indicator measures:
- Intentional transmissions (broadcasts, deliberate signals) that might reveal our existence or technology level
- Unintended RF leakage from communications infrastructure
- Interstellar messages or signals sent deliberately
- Compliance with information security regarding technosignature containment

### The Core Metric
```
Radio_Silence_Compliance = (Transmissions_With_Secrecy / Total_Transmissions) 칑 
                           (Unintended_Leakage_Containment / Total_Emissions_Risk)
```

**Assessment:**
- **Compliance > 0.9** = Transmissions are disciplined; minimal leakage (NOMINAL)
- **Compliance 0.6-0.9** = Some uncontrolled emissions; growing leakage risk (CAUTION)
- **Compliance < 0.6** = Multiple deliberate broadcasts; clear security failures (CRITICAL)

### Why This Matters
Unlike heat signatures which we must generate to power civilization, transmissions are largely optional. Every broadcast is a choice to reveal ourselves. Tracking compliance measures our collective discipline about information security.

## 4. Interpretation Guidance

### High Compliance (NOMINAL)
- **Meaning**: Civilization is maintaining radio discipline; transmissions are controlled
- **Implication**: We're not revealing ourselves through broadcasts
- **Action**: Maintain institutional discipline; protect against unauthorized transmissions

### Moderate Compliance (CAUTION)
- **Meaning**: Some transmissions are occurring; control is weakening
- **Implication**: We're at risk of revealing information through accumulation of signals
- **Actions**: 
  - Strengthen institutional controls on interstellar communication
  - Audit all external transmissions for security risk
  - Contain RF leakage through improved shielding
  - Reduce military RF emissions where possible
  - Educate public on risks of broadcasting
  - Establish formal decision process for any interstellar communication

### Low Compliance (CRITICAL)
- **Meaning**: Civilization is actively broadcasting; radio silence broken
- **Implication**: We have revealed our existence and/or technology level
- **Actions**: Trigger B1 emergency protocols
  - Immediate halt to all non-essential transmissions
  - Emergency RF shielding and containment
  - Preparation for external response
  - Unified global communication protocol
  - Contingency planning for detection and contact

## 5. Failure Modes & Limitations

### How This Indicator Could Be Misread

**False Positive Risk:**
- *Over-confidence in stealth*: Assuming undetected transmissions don't reveal us
- *Parochial focus*: Assuming military RF is only local concern when it's cosmic signal
- *Assuming compliance enforceability*: Radio discipline requires global coordination; easy to defect

**False Negative Risk:**
- *Hidden broadcasts*: Classified or unknown transmissions outside monitoring scope
- *Gradual norm drift*: Radio silence as value slowly erodes until suddenly broken
- *Cascading transmission*: Each additional broadcast makes previous broadcasts retroactively more dangerous

### How to Mitigate
1. **Multi-source verification**: Don't rely on single monitoring source
2. **Monitor global compliance**: Ensure discipline across all nations/factions
3. **Institutional memory**: Prevent erosion of radio silence as governing value
4. **Technological containment**: Engineer systems that cannot transmit without authorization
5. **Prepare for defection**: Assume someone will eventually try to broadcast
