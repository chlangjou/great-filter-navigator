## 1. Measurement Definition

### What We're Tracking
The **Ascension Readiness Index** measures:
- Overall preparedness for cosmic maturity and community membership
- Synthesis of alignment success, governance maturity, and value stability
- Evidence of readiness for "graduation" from quarantine
- Concrete metrics of ascension-level civilization development

### The Core Metric
```
Ascension_Readiness = (Alignment_Score 칑 0.4) + 
                      (Governance_Score 칑 0.3) + 
                      (Values_Stability_Score 칑 0.2) +
                      (Scientific_Maturity_Score 칑 0.1)
```

**Assessment:**
- **Readiness > 0.7** = Civilization prepared for cosmic ascension (NOMINAL)
- **Readiness 0.4-0.7** = Some preparation; needs improvement (CAUTION)
- **Readiness < 0.4** = Civilization not ready; development needed (CRITICAL)

### Why This Matters
This integrates B3 signals into a comprehensive readiness assessment. It guides both internal development and external communication about our cosmic maturity level.
