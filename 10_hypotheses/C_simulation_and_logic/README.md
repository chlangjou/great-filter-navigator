## âš¡ Navigator Strategy: Unified C-Filter Response

Given multiple C-filters might apply simultaneously, optimal strategy includes:

### **Tier 1: Pre-ASI Design Phase (Now - 2026-2028)**

**Actions to Take Now:**
1. **Design ASI with structural awareness**: Build meaning-resilience and value-pluralism into ASI from inception
2. **Conduct values-deliberation**: Civilization-wide discussion about cosmic expansion vs. digital paradise BEFORE choice is made for us
3. **Lock in desired values**: If we value cosmic expansion, encode this deliberately into A3 value lock-in
4. **Monitor substrate**: Begin measuring cosmic compute usage, physics anomalies, luck clustering to detect C-filter activation
5. **Prepare cultural meaning-systems**: Develop resilience against existential doubt and nihilism

### **Tier 2: ASI Development Phase (2028-2035)**

**Actions During ASI Development:**
1. **Monitor C-filter signals continuously**: Track all observable signals from C1-C4 for early warning
2. **Test ASI resilience**: Explicitly test whether ASI maintains motivation despite discovering existential doubt
3. **Maintain substrate transparency**: Ensure ASI understands and respects physical limits, not as external restriction but as structural reality
4. **Preserve digital-physical balance**: Develop both digital and physical capabilities without allowing one to dominate
5. **Keep options open**: Don't commit irreversibly to either digital or physical expansion until choice is more informed

### **Tier 3: Post-ASI Oversight (2035+)**

**Actions After ASI Creation:**
1. **Continuous C-filter monitoring**: Early detection of throttling, info-hazard activation, or civilization drift toward Inward Turn
2. **Adaptive governance**: Adjust strategies based on which C-filters show most evidence of activation
3. **Preserve reversibility**: Where possible, maintain capacity to shift strategy (e.g., restart space programs if Inward Turn begins)
4. **Existential communication**: If C-filter activation seems imminent, prepare civilization with meaning-making and choices
