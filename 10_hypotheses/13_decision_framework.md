## Part 6: Decision Authority Structure

### Three-Ring Governance Model

**Ring 1: Technical (ASI/AI Decisions)** - Hours to Days
- Authority: AI Safety Council (engineers + researchers)
- Decisions: Alignment, containment, operational details
- Veto: Any Ring 2/3 member can escalate

**Ring 2: Political (Governmental Decisions)** - Days to Years
- Authority: International Coordination Council
- Decisions: Resource allocation, strategy timing, B-C level choices
- Veto: Supermajority required (prevents single nation dominance)

**Ring 3: Civilizational (Cultural/Philosophical)** - Years to Centuries
- Authority: Democratic assemblies + value councils
- Decisions: Meaning, values, long-term civilization direction
- Veto: Permanent minority protections ensure pluralism

**Ring Interactions**:
- Ring 1 escalates conflicts to Ring 2
- Ring 2 escalates unresolved conflicts to Ring 3
- Ring 3 provides long-term guidance to Ring 2
- All rings feed input to Monitoring Dashboards
