## üéØ Purpose

This repository is a **catalog and decision notebook**, not a grand theory. Its purpose is to:
- Organize arguments and assumptions about civilization-scale risks.
- Make uncertainty, irreversibility, and failure modes explicit.
- Provide material that is **human-readable and machine-indexable**.
- Preserve reasoning trails so ideas can be revised rather than silently replaced.

## üåå Background and Motivation

This project was born from a need for **durable, gatekeeper-free knowledge artifacts**. 

Initial attempts to publish AI alignment analysis on traditional forums were restricted due to evolving policies on AI-assisted writing. This project pivots toward:
- **Durable artifacts** over ephemeral posts.
- **Transparent assumptions** over polished conclusions.
- **Open revision** via version control.

The working hypothesis is that structured knowledge is more robust under rapid change than forum-based discourse.
