# HOW TO EVALUATE: B1 Dark Forest Trigger

**Response Protocol:** Low-risk visibility and emergency stealth

---

## What to Measure

### 1. Evidence Quality (Quarterly)

**How:** Track UAP/contact claims by evidence grade: source, metadata, sensor fusion, chain-of-custody, independent review, and mundane alternatives.

**Success:** Claims remain clearly labeled as unresolved, confirmed mundane, confirmed anomalous, or confirmed contact.

### 2. Broadcast Governance (Continuous)

**How:** Monitor deliberate high-power interstellar transmissions, capability-revealing broadcasts, and unilateral contact attempts.

**Success:** No high-risk broadcast occurs without review and documented risk assessment.

### 3. Transparency Preservation (Semi-Annual)

**How:** Review whether B1 is being used to classify AI safety work, UAP evidence, or scientific findings beyond legitimate narrow security needs.

**Success:** Public-interest review and independent oversight remain intact.

### 4. Technosignature Calibration (Annual)

**How:** Update energy, heat, EM, and optical detectability thresholds using cited models and confidence intervals.

**Success:** Numerical tripwires remain provisional until source-backed and are not treated as hard policy boundaries.

### 5. Hostile-Contact Triggers (Urgent As Needed)

**How:** Watch for verified coercive, destructive, or threat-like behavior from a confirmed external actor.

**Success:** Emergency stealth is activated only when evidence crosses explicit hostile-risk thresholds.

---

## Warning Signs

- UAP records are presented as confirmed extraterrestrial contact without sufficient evidence.
- Strong secrecy becomes the default B1 response again.
- AI safety review is weakened by speculative external-threat claims.
- Private or national actors attempt unilateral high-risk broadcasting.
- Provisional thresholds such as 500 GW or 1 sigma IR variance are used without source-backed calibration.
- Verified hostile external behavior appears.

---

## Implementation

**Continuous:** Broadcast and hostile-contact incident monitoring
**Quarterly:** UAP/contact evidence quality review
**Semi-Annual:** Transparency and overclassification audit
**Annual:** Technosignature threshold calibration
**Urgent:** Emergency review if hostile-contact evidence appears

---

**Success** = Maintain low-risk visibility, avoid premature secrecy, preserve emergency stealth for hostile evidence, and keep B1 probability updates evidence-based.

---

**Evaluation Cycle:** Quarterly evidence review + annual governance audit
