## Decision Principles

**What we use to make framework evolution decisions:**

1. **Transparency** - All decisions documented with full rationale
2. **Community input** - Major weight given to community feedback
3. **Technical merit** - Solutions evaluated on architectural quality
4. **Backward compatibility** - Existing work preserved where possible
5. **Resource realism** - Decisions account for actual available resources
6. **Reversibility** - Where possible, decisions made reversible or deferrable

---

## Appeal Process

**If you disagree with a decision:**

1. Document specific concerns in writing
2. Tag issue [FRAMEWORK_DECISION_APPEAL]
3. Core team reviews appeal within 2 weeks
4. If new evidence presented, decision may be revisited
5. Final appeal to wider community if needed
